Welcome to Sensedroid!

We aim at making sensing more accessible to the DIY community. The Sensedroid projects focuses on providing a crowd sourced sensing platform based on the following components:

  • blueprints and tutorials on how to build your sensor board;
  • smartphone applications gathering data from the sensor board and providing live feedback;
  • a web platform for uploading and visualizing the sensor data.

Sensedroid sub-projects

Sensedroid is mainly the result of research at the IT University of Copenhagen and LMU Munich. Last year students from the ITU built a first proof of concept called NoxDroid
