Welcome to Sensedroid!

We aim to make sensing more accessible to the DIY community. The Sensedroid projects focus on providing a crowd sourced sensing platform based on the following components:

  • Sensors and boards (sensor recommendation, board blueprints and tutorials on how to build your own);
  • Smartphone applications, for aggregating sensor data and providing live feedback;
  • Web platform, for uploading and visualizing sensor data.

Sensedroid sub-projects

Sensedroid is mainly the result of research conducted by students of the IT University of Copenhagen and LMU Munich.

Last year, students from the ITU built a first proof of concept called NoxDroid (paper, poster). This year we are iterating on the concept with "SiNOx Sense - Simple NOx Sensing and Collection In The Wild" at the ITU, and improving SenseDroid hardware at the LMU.
